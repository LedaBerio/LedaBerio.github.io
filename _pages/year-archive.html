--- layout: archive permalink: /year-archive/ title: "Intersectional feminism: sources and links" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /wordpress/blog-posts/ --- I write and translate on Abbatto i Muri, an Italian intersectional feminist blog. You can find the blog here https://abbattoimuri.wordpress.com .
I recommend the following websites and pages for information and feminist education:
  • Feminist Killjoys is the blog by Sara Ahmed, independent feminist scholar
  • Linguistici is a blog about language and femist issues, written in Italian by linguistic scholars
  • Afrofeminas is an online magazine on black feminism (Spanish)
  • Feminism and Decolonisation is a very educational page on decolonisation and feminist issues
