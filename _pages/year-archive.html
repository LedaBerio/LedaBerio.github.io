You can find the blog here https://abbattoimuri.wordpress.com .
I have written in Italian on the media attacks to Greta Thunberg and how they are guided by a misogynist logic. You can find my article here . I recommend the following websites and pages for information and feminist education:
  • Feminist Killjoys is the blog by Sara Ahmed, independent feminist scholar
  • Linguistici is a blog about language and feminist issues, written in Italian by linguistic scholars
  • Afrofeminas is an online magazine on black feminism (Spanish)
  • Feminism and Decolonisation is a very educational page on decolonisation and feminist issues
